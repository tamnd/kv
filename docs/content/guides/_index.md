Each guide is built around a job rather than an API call: keeping writes consistent under concurrency, understanding the storage engine your data lands in, deciding how much durability to pay for, encrypting a database at rest, getting data safely off the box, serving it over a socket, and driving it from the shell. They assume you have worked through the [quick start](/getting-started/quick-start/).
